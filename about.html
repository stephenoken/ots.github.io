👋 Hej!

📍UX designer from Barcelona, based in Malmö. I’m passionate about the entire UX process and the Human center design.

After years as a Freelance Graphic designer I develop a love for typography and the use of colour and space to create harmonious layouts. But my seek to expand my knowledge by learning and creat more meaningful design drive me to transition in to UX design.

🎙 Being able to be the voice of the user and embrace the Build Empathy with you end user and understand their needs is the base for that have a real impact in their live.

My approach to UX design is following a solution based approach to solving problems.

📚 By day, I am constantly self-learning and developing skills in UX design to improve and deliver the best user experience.

In my free time, I like losing myself in an art museum, book store or a coffee shop.

Tack!
